SUBC: UBC's Student Powered Submarine Design Team

I first joined SUBC in my second year of university, and had the amazing opportunity to work as the team lead of SUBC's electrical steering team during my third year. SUBC is a diverse group of individuals from all different disciplines that works together to design and build a human-powered submarine and compete in international competitions.

The electrical steering system built for Skookumchuk Mk. IV included both manual and autonomous control. The pilot of the submarine was given manual control of the yaw of the submarine, while the pitch was autonomously controlled using a feedback loop. During my time with SUBC, I was able to see this project through from conceptualization and research all the way to a proof of concept.
